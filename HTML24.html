Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun. It is named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty. As the second-brightest natural object in Earth's night sky after the Moon, Venus can cast shadows and can be, on rare occasion, visible to the naked eye in broad daylight.A day on Venus is longer than a year.Venus is hotter than Mercury despite being further away from the Sun.Unlike the other planets in our solar system, Venus spins clockwise on its axis.Venus has 90 times the atmospheric pressure of Earth.
